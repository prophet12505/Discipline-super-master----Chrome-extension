Storytelling COMM-0398 (22.FA) M.Oliver

Since before the birth of civilization, human beings have used stories to understand and explain the world.

Today, stories are incorporated into many forms of informational communication including
podcasts, documentaries, news and sports reporting, and so on.

This course provides students with the foundational skills to craft original, compelling, and informative stories. This course also exposes students to aspects of indigeneity by exploring the history and importance of oral storytelling.
